⭐ Youtopia Animal Personality Campaign — Executive Summary

Launch an interactive Youtopia Animal Personality Test on Xiaohongshu (14-page swipe post), riding on the Zootopia 2 momentum. Objective: drive awareness → engagement → comments → community sharing → mini-program traffic → coupon redemption → orders.

2. Why This Campaign Works
	•	Addresses current issues: low XHS views, limited comments, weak awareness of the mini-program.
	•	Personality tests naturally trigger self-identification + commenting + sharing.
	•	Strong cultural relevance through Zootopia 2.
	•	Creates a low-cost yet high-impact funnel for real conversions.
