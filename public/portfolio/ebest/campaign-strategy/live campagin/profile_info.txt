Project: TikTok Live Campaign  
Company: eBest (Sydney-based retail company)  
Duration: 1 month  

Overview:
This campaign was initiated to grow our TikTok audience and explore live-streaming as a new channel for user acquisition in the Australian market.

Initially, our TikTok account had a limited follower base, and our first live stream focused on product introduction. The results were minimal, with only a few new followers.

We then shifted our approach by observing trending content patterns on TikTok — particularly “process-based” and repetitive visual content that audiences find satisfying and engaging.

Execution:
For the second phase, we set up a live stream in the warehouse environment, featuring a worker folding boxes in a continuous workflow. The large-scale warehouse setting, combined with the repetitive motion, created a visually engaging and calming viewing experience.

In later sessions, we further evolved the format by:
- introducing multiple participants
- adding light performative elements
- increasing the energy and pacing of the stream

Results:
- +500 followers within one month
- Improved live engagement compared to initial product-focused streams
- Identified a more effective content direction for audience growth

Key Insight:
Content that aligns with platform-native viewing behavior (e.g. satisfying, process-driven visuals) can significantly outperform traditional product-centric communication in driving engagement and growth.
